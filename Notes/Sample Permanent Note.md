# Sample Permanent Note

A developed idea that has earned a place in the permanent layer of the vault. Permanent notes are polished, stand on their own, and link to related ideas. They are what makes the Zettelkasten useful over time — the network of connections rather than any single note.

## Why Permanent Notes Matter

Permanent notes are the durable output of the workflow. Fleeting notes get captured, processed, and either discarded or promoted; literature notes distill external sources; permanent notes are where your own thinking lives.
