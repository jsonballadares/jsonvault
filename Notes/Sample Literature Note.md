# Sample Literature Note

Notes derived from an external source — a book, article, video, or course. The point of a literature note is to capture the source's ideas in your own words, with enough structure that your future self can use them without re-reading the original.

## Key Ideas

- First idea from the source, paraphrased
- Second idea, with any reaction or question you had while reading
- Third idea, connected to something else in the vault via [[Sample Permanent Note]]
